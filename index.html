About Us

Nila Sports Complex, established in December 2019 in western Tehran, spans an area of 800 square meters and is recognized as one of the finest women only fitness centers in the city. The complex began its official operations immediately after its founding, with a mission focused on empowering women through health, fitness, and community. Nila’s primary objectives are to provide a dedicated and fulltime space exclusively for women, to promote physical and mental well being, to expand and improve the quality of facilities for Iranian women, and ultimately, to contribute to enhancing the overall quality of society. Guided by the belief that “a woman true to the meaning of her name is the creator, nurturer, and blossom of life; improving her quality of life means improving the life of her family and community,” Nila Sports Complex embodies a holistic vision of women’s wellness. The facility includes a fully equipped gym, several group fitness halls, and advanced modern equipment sourced from the world’s leading brands. In addition to its fitness amenities, the complex also features a café, library, and spa areas, creating an inviting atmosphere where members can enjoy both recreation and relaxation.
